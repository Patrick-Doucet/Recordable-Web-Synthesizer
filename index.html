How to use:

There are two possible ways to play the Online Web Synthesizer:

  • You can click the notes directly with your mouse or touch screen device.

  • If you have access to a keyboard, you can use the keys on your keyboard associated to the piano, these are shown on the visual component.

Click the - or + buttons to change octaves or use the z/x keys.

To record: click the record button in the top right of the Web Synthesizer.

To see user recordings: Click here or click the Search tab.
